Team Edu

Team Edu is a group of friends, family, runners and cancer fighters dedicated to raising funds in memory of Eduardo O'Farrell (Edu), who lost his battle to cancer in December of 2016.

Donate

Team Edu is running, walking, cycling, swimming, and cheering to help those battling cancer, in memory of Edu. Funds will benefit research on life-saving cures at labs and research centers in the US, as well as treatment and patient support in Edu's home country of Argentina.

We are arranging the final details with our partner organizations: the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society in the US and Fundacion FLENI , where Edu received all his cancer treatment in Argentina. We will be ready to accept donations soon!
